Public speaking

Inclusive design - Accessibility as a design opportunity - February 2018

WIAD 2018 (Trento)

What happens when a design team is faced with accessibility regulations when designing a public digital service? We see how an apparent constraint has become an opportunity to build an inclusive design culture that goes beyond the provisions of the law, implementing it during the design and front-end development of the website of one of the main Italian airports.
